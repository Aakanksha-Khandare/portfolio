My top 3 movies of all time.

The Sixth sense


This is my favourite psychological thriller. It is about a boy who can see and talk to the dead and a psychiatrist who wants to heal this child.

Gone Girl


This movie is about finding out the reason of disappearence of Amy Dunne, leading to a major suspicion on her husband Nicholas Dunne. It is a movie filled with twists and turns and will keep you glued to your seat.

War


War is thrilling action movie with all sorts of bike and car stunts.

The Black Swan


This is anather psychological thriller that will keep you engrossed with its intense plot.
